History of the Club

NK Tomislav Livana was founded on April 1, 2005 by local residents of Livana, Briješće and Osijek's Cvjetno naselje. It was founded for the purpose of planned and organized development of the football branch in the settlements of Livana, Briješće, Osijek's Cvjetno naselje and beyond. The club represents a community-centered approach to football, fostering local talent and sportsmanship. In addition to its senior team, NK Tomislav Livana operates a School of Football for Kids, a vital initiative aimed at developing young talent and promoting a love for the sport among children.

The History of the Tournament

The "Milenko Jerković" Memorial Tournament is an annual international football competition organized by NK Tomislav Livana, dedicated to honoring the memory of one of its founders - Milenko Jerković. This event focuses on the Under-11 (U-11) age category, providing young athletes with the opportunity to compete and showcase their talents on an international stage. The most recent 16th edition took place on June 8-9, 2024, at the ŠRC Kralj Tomislav stadium in Livana. The event was well-received, with favorable weather conditions contributing to its success

The "Milenko Jerković" Memorial Tournament is more than just a football competition—it is a celebration of youth, talent, and the enduring spirit of sportsmanship. By bringing together young players from various countries, the tournament fosters international friendships, cultural exchange, and a shared passion for the beautiful game. It serves as a fitting tribute to Milenko Jerković’s memory, ensuring his legacy lives on through the joy and determination of every young athlete who takes the field.
